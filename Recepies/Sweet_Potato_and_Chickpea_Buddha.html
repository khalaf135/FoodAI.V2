Sweet Potato & Chickpea Buddha Bowl

Ingredients

For the Bowl:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 (15-ounce) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 cups spinach or kale, fresh
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Maple-Tahini Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Water, as needed to thin the dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Toss the diced sweet potatoes and chickpeas with olive oil, salt, and pepper, and spread them out on the baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the sweet potatoes are tender and the chickpeas are slightly crispy.
  4. While the sweet potatoes and chickpeas are roasting, prepare the maple-tahini dressing by whisking together the tahini, maple syrup, and lemon juice in a small bowl. Add water as needed to achieve a pourable consistency.
  5. To assemble the bowls, divide the cooked quinoa or rice between the bowls. Top with roasted sweet potatoes and chickpeas, avocado slices, and fresh spinach or kale.
  6. Drizzle with the maple-tahini dressing before serving.
