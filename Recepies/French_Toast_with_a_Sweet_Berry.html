French Toast with Sweet Berry Compote

Ingredients

For the French Toast:

  • 4 slices of thick bread (brioche or challah work well)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Butter for frying
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

For the Berry Compote:

  • 2 cups (290g) mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup (50g) sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

For the Berry Compote:

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine the berries, sugar, and lemon juice.
  2. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, until the berries break down and the sauce thickens, about 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. The compote will thicken as it cools.

For the French Toast:

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, allowing it to soak for a few seconds on each side.
  3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the soaked bread slices and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve the French toast warm, topped with the berry compote and a dusting of powdered sugar.
