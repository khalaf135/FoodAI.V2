No-Bake Peanut Butter Energy Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (100g) rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup (128g) natural peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup (113g) honey or agave syrup (for a vegan option)
  • 1/2 cup (60g) ground flaxseed
  • 1/2 cup (80g) mini dark chocolate chips (vegan if desired)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients thoroughly. The mixture should be slightly sticky but still manageable.
  2. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes. This will make it easier to handle.
  3. Roll the mixture into balls of about 1 inch (2.5 cm) in diameter.
  4. Store the energy balls in an airtight container and keep them refrigerated. They can also be frozen for long-term storage.
