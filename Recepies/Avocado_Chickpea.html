Avocado Chickpea Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Toast the whole-grain bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with a fork. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper, and mix well.
  3. Gently fold in the cooked chickpeas, trying not to crush them too much for a bit of texture.
  4. Spread the avocado and chickpea mixture evenly over the toasted bread slices.
  5. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and chopped parsley for an extra flavor kick.
  6. Enjoy your healthy and savory Avocado Chickpea Toast as a satisfying snack!
