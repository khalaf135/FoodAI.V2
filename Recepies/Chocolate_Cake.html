Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (400g) sugar
  • 3/4 cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
  • 1 cup (240ml) warm water
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, warm water, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. The batter will be thin.
  4. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting when the cakes have cooled completely.
